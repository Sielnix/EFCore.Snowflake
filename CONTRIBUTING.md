# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for considering contributing to this project!

By contributing, you agree to the terms listed below.

## License and Relicensing Agreement

By submitting a pull request or other contribution to this repository, you agree that:

- Your contribution is licensed under the same terms as the project **and**  
- You grant the project owner the right to **relicense your contribution under any license**, now or in the future, without requiring additional permission from you.

This includes (but is not limited to) changing the project license from LGPL-3.0 to MIT.

## How to Contribute

1. Fork the repository  
2. Create your feature branch  
3. Submit a pull request  
4. Confirm in a comment that you agree to the Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

Thank you for your contribution!
